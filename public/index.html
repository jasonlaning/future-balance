How much money will you have next year?

Planning for future spending would be simple (or at least easier) if every job paid monthly on the first of every month, and every bill or expense were due monthly on that same day. But that never happens!

Instead, planning ahead for expenses and income is complicated and often frustrating â€“ especially if you have a job that pays weekly or bi-weekly, but most of your expenses are monthly. FutureBalance helps you to:

  1. Use a dashboard to save income and expenses that you know will repeat regularly. Edit the entries at any time, or set a new current balance to create a new forecast.
  2. View a calendar report that calculates your daily balances for future dates, based on your current balance and any repeating income or expenses that you saved.
  3. View details for days on your calendar report that have an income or expense event, to quickly see what's causing your balance to change.
